# Field Notes

A dated log of what the room actually says in response to "hand back to the room" Fridays (see `00-Governance/DELIVERY_STRUCTURE.md`, section 2).

## Purpose

On a hand-back-to-the-room day, one real claim from the research is read to the crew along with a direct question: does this match your actual field experience? This folder is where the answer gets recorded — the same day, in plain language, no polish required.

This is not a summary and not a brief. It is a record of what people actually said, including disagreement, confusion, "that's not how it works for us," or flat contradiction of the claim. If the room pushes back on the research, that pushback is the valuable part of the entry — write it down as given, don't smooth it into agreement, and don't edit it later to make the research look more validated than it was.

## Format

One entry per hand-back-to-the-room day. Dated, three parts:

- **Date**
- **Claim presented** — the exact sentence read to the room that day
- **What the room said** — recorded the same day, in the crew's own words as closely as possible

## Template entry

```
## 2026-XX-XX

**Claim presented:** [one sentence — the exact claim read to the room]

**What the room said:**
[Plain-language record of the actual responses: agreement, disagreement, specific
examples from their own work, corrections to the claim, silence, disagreement among
themselves, whatever actually happened. Not smoothed over, not summarized into a
tidy conclusion if the room didn't give one.]
```
