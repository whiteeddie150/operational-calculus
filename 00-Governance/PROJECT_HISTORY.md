# Project History

## Origin

The project began as an attempt to improve daily safety briefs for PG&E field personnel.

Early discussions focused on weak signals, human performance, and front-line operational decision-making.

As discussions progressed, it became apparent that the emerging ideas extended beyond traditional safety and began to address broader questions of agency, judgment, adaptation, and participation in unfolding systems.

The repository was created on July 3, 2026, to preserve the evolution of these ideas and provide a durable research environment.
